Data Example Questions

Example Questions (Data Insights)

  • Provide a summary statistic of all numerical data!
  • Which are the 5 most expensive cars? Show type, price, hp, mileage, and fuel type!
  • Provide a pivot table with the average price by transmission type!

Example Questions (Graphics)

  • Show a histogram of prices!
  • Show the number of cars per 'fuel type' in a bar chart!
  • Plot the average price of the 10 most expensive makes in a bar chart. Arrange the prices in descending order!

Example Questions (Machine-Learning)

  • Fit a multiple linear regression model based on price (target), hp and mileage. Show a summary of the result.
  • Use k-means clustering based on the variables price, mileage and hp to find groups of cars.
  • Create a random forest regression based on price (target), mileage and hp only for the make VW. Predict the price of a VW with 120 hp and 65000 mileage.
